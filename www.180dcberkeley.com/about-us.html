    INTRODUCING OURSELVES

    Here at 180 Degrees Consulting, we are committed to providing affordable consulting services to the social sector and developing the next generation of social impact leaders. In line with UC Berkeley’s legacy of leading social change, our teams work passionately and diligently to develop practical solutions that increase our clients’ capacity to do good in the world.

    OUR VALUES

    We strive to strengthen all facets of the social sector by practicing cultural humility and taking an interdisciplinary, co-creative approach to solution-building. We understand that our clients are the experts, and we’re their biggest cheerleaders.

    Create social impact

    Grow our intellectual vitality 

    United by a drive for social change, we embrace a deep curiosity about the world around us. Our members are committed to continually expanding their intellectual horizons and striving towards the best versions of themselves.

    Take care of one another

    Beyond our work, we’re a close community that celebrates each others’ success and supports one another through hardship. These meaningful, long-lasting relationships are what make us a #180Fam.

    Build a more sustainable &  equitable world

    Social responsibility is at the center of everything we do. By working at the intersection of social good and business acumen, we do our part in movements towards a green economy and a just and prosperous society.

    OUR COMMUNITY

    Between caffeine-fueled work meetings and picnics on the Glade, 180DC is a tight-knit community of undergraduates united by ambition, passion, and hope. Cherishing our varied backgrounds, interests, and worldviews, we find strength in the diversity of our opinions and skill sets and are always there to celebrate each others’ success.

    OUR ALUMNI NETWORK

    The nature of our work and our extensive global network prepares our members to pursue meaningful careers in the world of consulting and beyond.
